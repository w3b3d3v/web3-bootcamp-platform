# WEB3DEV Bootcamp Platform
# https://build.w3d.community
# Last Updated: 2026-02-18

## Overview

WEB3DEV is a free online bootcamp platform focused on blockchain and Web3 education. We offer hands-on courses in smart contract development, DeFi, NFTs, and Web3 technologies. Upon course completion, students receive NFT-based certificates permanently stored on the Ethereum blockchain. Our mission is to make Web3 education accessible to everyone.

## Platform Information

- Platform Type: Free educational bootcamp
- Primary Language: Portuguese (pt-BR)
- Secondary Language: English (en)
- Target Audience: Developers learning Web3 and blockchain technologies
- Certificate System: NFT-based completion certificates on Ethereum (soulbound/non-transferable)
- License: MIT Open Source
- Repository: https://github.com/w3b3d3v/web3-bootcamp-platform

## Key Pages

### Main Pages

- /courses/[id] — Individual course pages

## Technology Stack

- Framework: Next.js 12 (React 17)
- Database: Firebase (Firestore + Auth)
- Blockchain: Ethereum (NFT certificates via thirdweb SDK)
- Styling: TailwindCSS + NextUI
- Deployment: Vercel

## NFT Certificates

WEB3DEV issues NFT-based completion certificates on the Ethereum blockchain. These certificates are:
- Non-transferable (soulbound) — permanently linked to the student's wallet
- Verifiable on-chain via Etherscan or OpenSea
- Free to claim — WEB3DEV covers all gas fees
- Usable on LinkedIn under "Licenses & Certifications"
